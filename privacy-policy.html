Privacy Policy

At Wynelle Daily Grocery, we are committed to earning your trust by safeguarding your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you shop with us, both online and in store.

Information We Collect

To provide you with a smooth and convenient shopping experience, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details: Name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • Order details: Items purchased, delivery instructions, and payment information.
  • Communication records: Messages, emails, and feedback you send us.
  • Website usage data: Information about your device, browser, and how you interact with our website (for improving our service).

How We Use Your Information

  • To process, fulfill, and deliver your orders safely and efficiently.
  • To communicate with you about your order, promotions, and important updates.
  • To improve our products, services, and customer experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our business and customers from fraud or misuse.

How We Protect Your Privacy

Your privacy matters to us. We store your information on secure systems and restrict access to authorized personnel only. We never sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Any sharing of data (such as with delivery partners or payment providers) is done strictly for the purpose of completing your order and is protected by confidentiality agreements.

Your Choices & Rights

  • You may access, update, or correct your personal information by contacting us directly.
  • You can opt out of promotional messages at any time.
  • If you wish to have your data removed from our system, please let us know and we will fulfill your request except where retention is required by law.

Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website uses cookies and analytics tools to understand user preferences and improve your browsing experience. You can adjust your browser settings to limit or block cookies if you prefer.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about your privacy, please reach out to us at contact@wynellegrocery.com or call +63 926 487 9034. We are here to assist you.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

Thank you for choosing Wynelle Daily Grocery. Your privacy and trust are very important to us.
